Slug 2023
Sri Lanka University Games (SLUG | 2023) on 8th July to 8th September

Inter-University sports championships have been organized since the 1950s. The first Sri Lanka University Games (SLUG) was held in 1980 at the University of Peradeniya. The goal of SLUG is to place sports and physical education at the service of the harmonious development of Sri Lankan universities, with a view to promoting a peaceful society concerned with the preservation of human dignity. It covers 16 state universities of Sri Lanka comes under the purview of the University Grants Commission. Over 6,000 players will participate in 24 games including 40 events for men and women. The SLUG is a highly anticipated event held every three years in rotation among Sri Lankan State Universities, bringing together athletes from all over the country. Hosting this event is a unique opportunity for our university and the entire Island to promote sports development and a peaceful society. The Eastern University, Sri Lanka takes pride in hosting the distinguished 14th Sri Lanka University Games (SLUG).

Participation: 4000+ students

Overall, the ITUM Staff Team won a total of five medals in the tournament, showcasing their exceptional skills and dedication to the sport.

ITUM Base ball Club

31st March, 2022 at the National Baseball Stadium, Diyagama.According to a concept of Director of ITUM Major General (Retired) S.K. Thirunavukarasu RSP VSV USP, an introductory baseball coaching camp was organized by ITUM Sports and Physical education unit in collaboration with the SL Army Baseball team. Basic Introduction on Baseball was given by National Baseball Coach Mr. Iresh Kosala, and Army Baseball captain Sergeant Dissanayake gave their fullest corporation in conducting practical sessions with his team. At the end of the program, as expected, the hidden talents of ITUM students were discovered. Besides that, new friendships were formed between ITUM and SL Army baseball teams as a result of this program.

ITUM Staff Badminton tournament

"IT'S Bad To" (ITUM Staff Badminton Tournament) 2022 organized by the Staff Welfare Society was successfully held on the 16th of February 2022 at the indoor stadium of ITUM. As the honored chief guests of the event, Mr. Sachin Dias and Ms. Thilini Hendahewa, the badminton mixed double champions of Sri Lanka, who made our country proud and victorious by winning the gold medal in badminton mixed double at ODISHA Open 2022 recently held in India took part to witness the badminton skills of the ITUM staff. More than 30 staff members participated as contestants in the tournament, and everyone enjoyed the event to the maximum. With the support of two referees from UOM, Mr. Lakmal & Mr. Pavithra the contestants were randomly divided into 4 teams with 7-8 participants for each team. Opponent teams had to play five matches including one women's double, two men's doubles, and two mixed doubles matches. The director of the institute also participated as a contestant, and they were able to secure the championship under the great leadership of Mr. Kajaharan. Mr. Amjath and his team became the 1st runners-up while Mr. Sanjaya and his team became the 2nd runners-up. No matter what, as we expected at the end of the day everyone won because everyone enjoyed the event, the hidden talents were discovered, and new friendships were developed among the staff of the ITUM.

Participation: 40+ staff Members
